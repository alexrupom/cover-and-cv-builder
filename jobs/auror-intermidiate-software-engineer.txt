About Auror
At Auror, we’re empowering the retail industry to tackle theft and Organised Retail Crime, a $150 Billion problem globally. It’s high volume crime that’s increasingly organised in nature and is putting people, retailers, and communities at risk every day.

Founded in New Zealand 13 years ago, we’re working with some of the best and largest retailers in the world across the US, Canada, Australia, New Zealand, and the UK.

Auror is connecting people and intelligence to reduce crime. We’re using technology for good.

In partnership with our leading retail partners, we need people with the passion, determination, and innovation required to overcome one of the world's largest problems. If you’re looking to make a difference with and for the people dedicated to stopping crime, for good, then we want you on our team.

We're also embracing the potential of AI to supercharge our impact—whether that's enhancing the way we detect trends, support our customers, or improve internal workflows. As a company, we're committed to responsibly incorporating AI into how we work and what we build, and we encourage all Aurors to be curious about how AI can elevate their work, regardless of role or function.

About Auror Engineering

Collaboration is at the heart of the Engineering team at Auror. We believe that we can only build the very best software by working with each other and seeking different perspectives to solve problems. We’re a team that:

Ship code to production constantly: Our product team ships code to production on average at least 10-15 times a day prioritising small releases and short feedback cycles and we do this with a minimal defect rate.

Work on cloud native architecture: We focus on delivering business value to customers, without the burden of maintaining complex infrastructure

Work on innovative and up-to-date tech stacks: We are constantly evolving our technology stack inline with the needs of our business, leveraging React and Typescript for front-end and C# / Dotnet for back end. We host our services in Azure and utilise a wide range of cloud-native services with an evolving service-oriented architecture.

Contribute to our product: We work with Product Management and Product Design from the very beginning to make sure we’re delivering value to our customers and making a meaningful impact.

Supports a coaching culture: Together, we support one another to level up

Continuously improve: We are relentless about getting better and improving our processes, tooling and tech stack.

The Role

We’re looking for a full stack Software Engineer to join our Actionable Intelligence stream in Product Engineering. In this role, you’ll help evolve our Insights experience, building features that enable customers to explore data, uncover patterns, and turn complex information into clear, actionable outcomes.

This is not a traditional dashboarding problem. You’ll work on a product that brings together multiple data sources in the retail crime environment and helps customers make sense of them. You’ll also contribute to innovative solutions that introduce more flexible, agent-like experiences, enabling users to ask questions of their data rather than rely solely on dashboards.

You’ll contribute across frontend and backend systems, taking well-defined problems from idea through to production, while continuing to build your technical depth, judgment, and independence in a supportive team environment.

Location

This role is Auckland based.

Practically this will involve:

Shipping customer-facing features that improve how users explore, understand, and act on data

Owning delivery end-to-end, from shaping the approach with your team through to build, test, release, and production support

Building clean, responsive UI in React + TypeScript, with attention to usability, performance, and real-world workflows

Designing and extending backend services and APIs in C# / .NET

Working with data-driven systems and contributing to how insights are surfaced in the product

(Bonus) Exposure to tools like Cube/CubeJS and Snowflake, though prior experience isn’t required

Making pragmatic decisions in a cloud-native environment (Azure & GCP), balancing performance, reliability, and cost

Breaking work into small, iterative increments so value is delivered continuously

Debugging issues across the stack using logs, metrics, and user feedback

Writing maintainable, well-tested code that the team can build on

Collaborating closely with engineers, product, and design through pairing, reviews, and shared problem solving

You’ll be reporting to Georgia Barnett, Engineering Lead
