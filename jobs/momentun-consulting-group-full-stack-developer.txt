About the job
This is a great opportunity to join a tight-knit technology team at the heart of a well-established retail business, where your work will have real visibility and genuine impact.

The Environment

The team operates with a flat structure and an agile mindset, meaning you'll move quickly, work across a varied backlog, and interact regularly with senior stakeholders. If you like being close to the business and seeing the direct outcomes of your work, this is that kind of role.

AI is actively used within the development practice to accelerate delivery, and the team is exploring further automation and data interfacing use cases. An open mind toward AI tools is a must.

The Role

This is a 12-month fixed-term contract with high potential to extend further. Your initial focus will be approximately 80% backend development, working through a meaningful development backlog and increasing overall output within the team.

This is not a mentoring position. You need to be confident in your fundamentals and ready to contribute from day one.

Must-have's

Strong backend development experience using Laravel and PHP
Front-end capability in React
Solid skills in SQL and SQL querying
Exposure to, or, working knowledge of Power BI
Strong communication with an excellent grasp of written and spoken English

Nice to have's:

Comfortable working in legacy system environments and understanding integration constraints
A genuine interest in AI tooling and its practical application in development

Working Environment: This role is based in the Auckland CBD, offering hybrid working and carparking on-site (which is available on a rostered basis). The team offers flexibility around in-office hours to support personal commitments.

Benefits include KiwiSaver contributions and significantly discounted rates on company products and services for you and your family.

Ready to Apply? If this sounds like the kind of role where you can do your best work, we would love to hear from you. Get in touch with your CV and a brief note on what draws you to this opportunity.

Please note we can only consider applicants currently based in NZ with valid working rights. We cannot offer accreditation for this role.
